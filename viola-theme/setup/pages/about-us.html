Who we are

Founded in 2011, Viola Hair Extensions is a premium supplier of high-quality hair extensions to the hairdressing and beauty industries — and Viola Hair Extensions Canada is our Canadian branch, bringing the same hair, standards and support to Canadian stylists and salons.

We pride ourselves on helping everyone who seeks to enhance or transform their look to find just that — hair extensions that give the wow factor!

What's important to us

We supply our European Remy hair extensions in a wide variety of types, colours, lengths and weights, together with the tools and techniques to help propel your salon to the top of the industry.

Our trade customers are extremely important to us and we work closely with salons to offer support and encouragement to build their business. Your success is our success, and we work together to achieve maximum growth.

We believe that love is in the hair — so let's work together and raise the profile of perfect hair extensions.

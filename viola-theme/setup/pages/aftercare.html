Viola Hair Extensions come with a 190-day Quality Guarantee. It has been proven that if you follow the aftercare, have regular maintenance and use Viola specially formulated aftercare products, our hair will last even longer than 190 days — 360 days or even more!

For your extensions to last looking and feeling strong, please be faithful to the aftercare advice.

Washing

To keep your hair extensions healthy and looking luxurious use Viola Hair Extension Shampoo and Conditioner — our products have been specially formulated to coat, seal, help restore and add shine.

  • To ensure the extensions do not become matted, detangle your hair before washing.
  • To reduce pulling and tangles, keep your head upright when washing. Do not rub or twist your hair — massage the scalp gently with your fingertips.
  • Avoid putting conditioner onto the bonds as it can cause the beads or glue to slip out of the hair.
  • After washing, gently squeeze out the excess water and pat the hair dry. Do not wrap your hair in a towel, as this will cause matting.

Brushing & blow-drying

  • Use the correct hair extension brush — a bristle or detangle brush — with the right technique to reduce pressure on the bonds and roots.
  • Hair will become damaged when brushed while it is still wet.
  • Once the hair is completely dry, brush starting from the ends and work your way up towards the roots, supporting the hair by holding the bonds.

Styling

  • The extensions can be straightened, curled and blow-dried — but frequent heat exposure will impact their lifespan, as it would your own hair.
  • Keep heat tools away from the bonds — direct contact will cause the bonds to melt and become fragile.
  • Protect against heat with Viola Shake & Protect or Viola Thermal Protection Serum.
  • To maintain a glossy sheen, use Viola Extension Boost, Viola Hair Food and Viola Argan Oil (brunettes only) regularly to replenish essential oils and vitamins.

Dyeing / colouring

Bleaching or dyeing will shorten the lifespan of your extensions. If you choose to colour, ensure it is carried out by a professional — results may vary due to the processes the extensions have already undergone. Altering the extensions in this way will invalidate the guarantee.

Sleeping

Before going to bed, make sure the hair is:

  • Completely dry — wet hair will become matted.
  • Brushed and braided into one plait.

Holidays & swimming

Take extra care around chlorine, salt water, UV and heat — they can change the colour of light hair and leave extensions dry and brittle.

  • Cover hair when exposed to direct sunlight.
  • Keep sunscreen and oils away from your extensions — they can turn the hair pink/orange. Wear hair up in a bun so it does not touch skin where sunscreen has been applied.
  • Chlorine and salt water can turn hair green. Wear hair up out of the water; if it does get wet, wash immediately with Viola Extension Shampoo and Conditioner.
  • If you notice discolouration, a small amount of Viola Purple Shampoo may counteract brassy tones — test on a small section first.

Maintenance

Maintenance is required every 4 to 12 weeks (depending on the type of extensions) to avoid damaging your own hair and to check all is well.

For same-day dispatch, orders must be placed before 2 pm ET Monday to Friday (excluding statutory holidays). Orders placed after the cut-off will be dispatched the next business day. We do not ship on weekends or statutory holidays.

Delivery estimates are always estimates. Viola Hair Extensions Canada reserves the right to change delivery services to ensure the correct service is chosen for your area and order value. Most items must be signed for.

Product returns

You may cancel your order for any reason up to the point of dispatch and any payments will be refunded in full. However, most orders ship within just a few hours — once in transit, an order cannot be cancelled or refused unless instructed by us in writing.

Please inspect your goods immediately upon receipt and notify us by email within 7 days if the goods are damaged or do not match your order.

Hair extensions are hygiene-sensitive goods. Once such products have been unsealed and used, they are no longer eligible for return or refund.

If you change your mind, you may return unused goods within 14 days of delivery:

  • Returns must include a completed Viola Returns form.
  • Goods must be returned at your expense within 28 days of cancellation, unused and in "as new" condition with all original packaging and seals intact.
  • Tape tabs must be returned with all Tape-In and Secret Tape-In extensions; otherwise the cost of the tabs will be deducted from the refund.
  • Refunds cover the goods only, excluding delivery charges. Used or tampered products will not be refunded.
  • Returns with damaged or missing packaging may incur a repackaging fee.

Non-returnable: sale/clearance items, wigs, bespoke items, and online courses once digital access has been provided.

Damaged goods

Where a claim of damage is made, return the goods within 28 days of delivery. If the goods are found to be damaged you will be entitled to a replacement or a full refund (including delivery costs) plus return postage.

For courier claims (denial of receipt, damage, lost parcel), a refund or replacement will be processed after the courier has investigated and confirmed the claim.

Trade customers receive

  • Exclusive discounts
  • Access to our trade-exclusive Luxe range
  • Best value on hair extensions, tools and more
  • Excellent deals and offers
  • First to know about new products and upcoming promotions
  • Opportunities to increase your discount
  • Excellent service & support

How to apply

Create an account on our website and send a copy of your hair extension certificate (must be from an industry-recognised training organisation) to canadasupport@violahairextensions.co. Once the account has been verified and accepted it will be upgraded — each time you log in, trade prices will show automatically.

Please note: a hair extensions qualification is required as proof that a course has been passed and completed.

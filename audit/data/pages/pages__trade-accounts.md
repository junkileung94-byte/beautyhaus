TRADE ACCOUNTS

At Viola, we want to give our trade customers the best possible value to help share our successes together. A Viola trade account will help to reduce costs and increase margins.

Trade customers receive:

Exclusive discounts 
Access to our Trade Exclusive Luxe range!
Best value on hair extensions, tools, etc. 
Excellent deals and offers
Be the first to know about new products and upcoming promotions 
Opportunities to increase their discount 
Excellent service & support
HOW TO APPLY

Just create an account on our website where you can attach a copy of your Hair Extension certificate (must be from an industry recognised training organisation), once the account has been verified and accepted it will then be upgrade. 

Each time you log onto your account, the prices will automatically change.  

Please be aware to be eligible for a Viola Hair Extensions Trade account, a hair extensions qualification is required as proof that a course has been passed and completed.

If you would like any more information please contact us support@violahairextensions.co.uk

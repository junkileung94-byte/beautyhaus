ABOUT US
WHO WE ARE

Founded in Romsey in 2011 by the company’s proud name sake, Viola Hair Extensions the UK’s premium supplier of high-quality hair extensions to the hairdressing and beauty industries. Not only do we supply but we train our clients to help them bring to their clients the very best service available.

We pride ourselves on being able to help everyone who seeks to enhance or transform their look to find just that – hair extensions that give the wow factor!

Viola is well established and respected within the industry with business offices, warehousing and a demonstration salon and a dedicated team waiting to hear from you. We only supply the best as we know only the best is good enough for our clients.

WHAT'S IMPORTANT TO US

We supply our European Remy hair extensions in a wide variety of types, colours, lengths and weights, together with tools and now the techniques to help propel your salon to the top of the industry. We have all the products and experience to help you ensure that you are able to help your clients achieve their ultimate look.

Our trade customers are extremely important to us and we work closely with salons to offer support and encouragement to build their business…Your success is our success and we work together to achieve maximum growth.

We believe that love is in the hair so let’s work together and raise the profile of perfect hair extensions.

Product Returns

The Buyer may cancel the order for any reason up to the point of dispatch and any payments made by the Buyer shall be refunded in full within 28 days. However, most of the time we ship your order within just a few hours. Once they are in route to you, the order cannot be cancelled or refused unless instructed by us in writing. If you do refuse it, you will be responsible for charges of shipping and handling both ways and an additional restocking fee.
The Buyer shall inspect the Goods immediately upon receipt and shall notify the Seller by e-mail within 7 days if the Goods are damaged or do not comply with any of the Contract. If the Buyer fails to do so the Buyer shall be deemed to have accepted the Goods.

Hair extensions are classified as hygiene-sensitive goods. In line with UK law, once such products have been unsealed and used, they are no longer eligible for return or refund.

Under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, Schedule 9, paragraph 13(c):
“The consumer loses the right to cancel the contract when the goods are sealed for health protection or hygiene reasons and have been unsealed after delivery.”

Where a claim of damage is made, the Goods shall be returned by the Buyer to the Seller within 28 days of delivery. The Buyer shall be entitled to a replacement or a full refund (including delivery costs) plus any return postal charges if the Goods are in fact damaged.
If you are a consumer. We want you to be delighted every time you shop with us and know that we all change our minds sometimes! If you don’t like something you have bought from us you have the right, in addition to your other rights, to cancel the Contract and return the Goods for any reason within 14 days of taking the delivery. 

Returns must include a completed Viola Returns form to be included with the returned goods.

Goods must be returned by the Buyer at the Buyer’s expense within 28 days of cancellation in an unused and ‘as new’ condition with all the original packaging, and if applicable, all seals in-tact. The Buyer will receive a refund of all monies paid for the Goods excluding delivery charges. A refund or exchange will not be provided for products returned in a used condition or been tampered with.

Tape tabs must be returned with all Tape-in and Secret Tape-in hair extensions. If the tapes are not returned with the hair the cost for the tabs will be deducted from the refund.

All packaging must be unmarked, undamaged, untampered with, and returned in the same condition as received. Any returns received with damaged, marked, tampered, or otherwise non-compliant packaging may be subject to a processing and repackaging fee, which will be deducted from any refund due.
When you return your item to us, please make sure that it’s well packed to protect it from damage. Where returned Goods are found to be damaged due to the Buyer’s fault the Buyer will be liable for the cost of remedying such damage.
Online courses are non-refundable once access to the digital content has been provided. If the Buyer wishes to return a physical course kit, the Seller must be notified in writing prior to the kit or online content being dispatched or issued.
SALE items/CLEARANCE, Wigs and Bespoke items are NON-returnable.

International Delivery

It’s easy to buy from us if you do not live in the UK! And with our simple pricing schedule, it’s cost-effective too! Enjoy our low prices – wherever you are! Once you are ready to buy, select your country at the checkout, and our site will automatically apply the delivery charges.

Your order will be delivered on a DAP (“Delivery At Place”) service. This means UK VAT is removed from your order by us at checkout, but you will be liable for your local VAT and any duty prior to receiving your parcel. This will be estimated in UK pounds but will be payable in local currency and may be subject to exchange rate fluctuations.

Please note that without payment of this your order will not be released from customs into your country and may be returned to Viola Hair Extensions. Payment of local VAT and duty is your responsibility and not included in the price that you pay to Viola Hair Extensions.

It is your responsibility to reclaim any local VAT and duty paid should you decide to return your order under our returns policy.

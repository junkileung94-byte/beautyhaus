WHY VIOLA ACADEMY?

Our Viola Academy hair extension training has been created for the benefit of stylists that want to extend their knowledge. Our professional hair extension training is tailored to include Nano tip, Tape-in, Beaded Weave & Eaziweave. These categories can be tailored for a complete beginner, or for an experienced hairdresser.

Our hair extension training courses are held over 1 or 2 days sessions where you can gain full certification. We take great pride in what we do and promise to provide the finest training to ensure that our students have the best knowledge and skills needed to become an approved and certified Extensionist.

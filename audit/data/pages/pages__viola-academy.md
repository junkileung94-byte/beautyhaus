VIOLA ACADEMY

Welcome to the Viola Academy, the ultimate destination for hair extension education. Whether you're a seasoned stylist or just starting out, our comprehensive training courses will equip you with the skills and knowledge you need to succeed in the world of hair extensions.

At Viola, we are looking for like-minded people to participate in our hair extension training courses. Are you looking to offer something different to your clients that will help to increase your income? Do you want to develop and learn new skills, or even start a new business adventure?
Our Viola Academy hair extension training has been created for the benefit of stylists that want to extend their knowledge. Our professional hair extension training is tailored to include Nano tip, Tape-in, Beaded Weave. These categories can be tailored for a complete beginner, or for an experienced hairdresser.

Viola Academy has 3 ways in which you can train to be an exceptional hair extensionist…
We have our in-house training here at Viola Academy Centre in Romsey, near Southampton.
Also available is our in-salon training where our educator comes to your salon, and now we have our brand new online courses, where you can learn in the comfort of your own home.
All 3 methods (Nano, Tape-ins and Beaded Weave) are available however you decide to learn.

At the Viola Academy, we believe that education is the key to success. That's why we offer courses designed to meet the needs of stylists. Our expert instructors have years of experience in the hair extension industry and are committed to helping you achieve your goals.
